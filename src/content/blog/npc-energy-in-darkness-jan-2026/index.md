## From the National Political Committee — Energy in Darkness

Dear Comrades,

Happy New Year! Just a few weeks into 2026, we're feeling how intensely the contradictions are heightening across our society.

On New Year's Day, we celebrated with our comrades in New York City who [froze their toes off](https://www.democracynow.org/2026/1/2/mamdani_inauguration_vox_pop) for hours in the crowd of thousands outside City Hall, [inspired by the warmth of collectivism](https://jacobin.com/2026/01/zohran-mamdani-collectivism-rugged-individualism-inauguration-speech) as we [watched the inauguration of Zohran Mamdani,](https://www.youtube.com/live/iazY_UDQdck?si=I9MtyxoHybGLrU14) who proudly announced he will [govern as a democratic socialist](https://www.instagram.com/p/DS_EHUqEpwt) mayor of the world's wealthiest city. While Lucy Dacus [sang the historic labor anthem "Bread and Roses,"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ftVpyoSUpt8) [Bernie Sanders swore in](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=suPQpS-JY4Q) the mayor [over a Quran,](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/1/1/zohran-mamdani-sworn-in-as-new-york-mayor-with-historic-quran) and Zohran himself [touted "DSA meetings" as a core part of the civic fabric](https://www.nyc.gov/mayors-office/news/2026/01/mayor-zohran-mamdani-inaugural-address) of this city of 8 million people, it was hard not to feel deeply moved by [how far our movement has come](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/01/02/style/mamdani-paragon-brooklyn-democratic-socialist.html) through the past decade of DSA's massive growth in the United States.

Within just two days, we watched in horror as the Trump administration bombed Caracas and rapidly escalated an [illegal military intervention and regime change in Venezuela](https://www.dsausa.org/statements/dsa-condemns-trumps-illegal-war-against-venezuela-dsa-condena-la-guerra-ilegal-de-trump-contra-venezuela/) by kidnapping their President and his wife. Just days after that, [ICE agents murdered Renee Nicole Good](https://www.dsausa.org/statements/twin-cities-dsa-on-ice-agents-murder-of-renee-good/) in Minneapolis, and we continue to see an all-out siege of the Twin Cities by the occupying force of ICE. [You can donate here to support Twin Cities DSA's ICE Watch and Know-Your-Rights work.](https://secure.actblue.com/donate/iceoutmn) Threats of active regime change are [escalating against Cuba,](https://www.dsausa.org/statements/dsa-condemns-trump-and-rubios-threats-against-cuba-in-the-aftermath-of-the-us-coup-in-venezuela-dsa-condena-las-amenazas-de-trump-y-rubio-contra-cuba-tras-el-intento-de-golpe-de-estado-estado/) and Trump is increasing belligerence against Mexico, Colombia, Greenland, Nigeria, Iran, and more — openly invoking the Monroe Doctrine of naked imperialism from over a century ago, and shamelessly embracing it as the ["Donroe Doctrine"](https://www.thenation.com/article/society/donroe-doctrine-venezuela-maduro/) to dominate the whole hemisphere to plunder resources.

Next week is Martin Luther King, Jr. Day. His internationalist vision is more crucial than ever. In his pivotal ["Beyond Vietnam" speech](https://www.americanrhetoric.com/speeches/mlkatimetobreaksilence.htm), Dr. King said "A nation that continues year after year to spend more money on military defense than on programs of social uplift is approaching spiritual death." Today, the US is in a [zombie state.](https://ips-dc.org/martin-luther-king-jr-s-internationalist-vision-is-more-crucial-than-ever/) Our government's military budget soars past $1 trillion, and the ICE budget balloons larger than the militaries of most other countries — all while basic social services are cut for millions of working class people.

As Antonio Gramsci said: "The old world is dying, and the new world struggles to be born; now is the time of monsters." Against all the darkness, we believe [this is our time to win.](https://www.youtube.com/live/FoM1Phq6G64) Against the flailing of the monsters leading this regime, we're organizing everywhere for a [gorgeous and more compassionate world for all.](https://www.instagram.com/p/DSMAJGBjNp4) Millions of people are realizing what we've known all along: as the existing order continues to decay, socialism is what can beat fascism.

Across the country, that's what DSA is demonstrating. Within hours of Trump's invasion of Venezuela, our National Political Committee sprang into action with our members to coordinate days of action and say [Hands Off Venezuela.](https://www.youtube.com/live/-Dnj7Oi8txg) [DSA chapters quickly organized actions](https://www.instagram.com/p/DTTGZYZjjUZ/) and showed up with thousands across the country to say No War, No ICE. More and more Americans are [realizing that abolishing ICE isn't a radical demand.](https://today.yougov.com/politics/articles/53878-more-americans-view-ice-shooting-minnesota-unjustified-than-justified-january-9-12-2026-economist-yougov-poll) It's a rational response to an out-of-control force of state terror that's [still younger](https://www.wbur.org/hereandnow/2018/06/25/immigration-abolish-ice) than most DSA members today.

MLK Day will fittingly kick off a week of protest across the country, with labor unions and community groups gearing up for a [statewide shutdown of Minnesota.](https://inthesetimes.com/article/minneapolis-renee-good-ice-shooting-labor-unions) **We're calling on people across the country to join a nationwide day of action against ICE terror on January 20,** [**to walk out**](https://www.womensmarch.com/) **and** [**show up on the streets.**](https://www.instagram.com/p/DTToTZgkX9D/#)

From coast to coast, chapters are taking on Trump's fascist deportation machine. DSA organizers from Los Angeles to Chicago to Charlotte are learning from each others' strategies to [help each other protect residents and resist ICE](https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/dec/16/ice-immigration-raids-cities) — participating in ICE Watch programs, exposing the malfeasance of ICE agents near Minneapolis as they [swapped out license plates](https://x.com/TwinCitiesDSA/status/2001039294509568201) before conducting raids to kidnap people, organizing for sanctuary city legislation, and making [ICE collaboration a toxic decision](https://www.californiadsa.org/news/avelo2025sep) for businesses like Avelo Airlines. Within a day of Renee's killing by ICE, we learned that DSA members helped score a major win to [pressure Avelo to cancel their contract with ICE,](https://truthout.org/articles/heres-how-we-pressured-an-airline-to-end-its-contract-with-ice/) after chapters across the country [threw down over the past year with a boycott campaign](https://www.instagram.com/p/DTQSVDhjrTm/#) to expose their deportation flights and show that tearing families apart is toxic to their bottom line.

[Learn more about that incredible win on our mass call on Wednesday 1/21 at 8pm ET/7pm CT/6pm MT/5pm PT: From Solidarity with Minneapolis to a Win Against Avelo Airlines!](https://actionnetwork.org/events/ice-out-from-solidarity-with-minneapolis-to-a-win-against-avelo-airlines) [And then join us for our next ICE OUT call](https://actionnetwork.org/events/fight-fascism-build-socialism-dsa-mass-call-4/) Tuesday 2/3 at 7pm ET/6pm CT/5pm MT/4pm PT!

Meanwhile, DSA chapters across the country are standing with workers against bad bosses. [From Atlanta,](https://atlantaciviccircle.org/2025/12/09/starbucks-strike-community-support-atlanta-baristas/) [to Detroit,](https://medium.com/dsa-detroit-newspaper/brewed-for-solidarity-dsa-starbucks-support-gains-steam-ec57528d31f9) [to Denver,](https://coloradotimesrecorder.com/2025/11/latte-less-picketers-persuade-starbucks-customers-to-exit-the-drive-thru-without-buying-anything/74778/) [to Wilmington in North Carolina,](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DSTFhECkdOM/) [to San Antonio](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DSOVT0dEUnG/) in Texas, and [beyond,](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DSXf2UuDfgb/) are standing with [Starbucks Workers United](https://sbworkersunited.org/our-strike/) and saying ["No contract? No coffee!"](https://starbucksworkersunited.controlshift.app/petitions/no-contract-no-coffee-pledge-to-act-in-solidarity-with-starbucks-workers-united?utm_source=dsa-website) Last month, striking baristas [brought the picket line](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DSSxX8YD6b0/) directly to Starbucks' biggest regional distribution centers [in northern Nevada](https://x.com/SBWorkersUnited/status/2000568781454950570) [and Pennsylvania,](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DSSoLIQCJ2q/) and [DSA labor organizers](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DSSg1uYDl56/) and [chapter leaders](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DSSdZ_VjvVj/) [showed up in solidarity](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DSSe4ohjtsW/) [as they faced mass arrest](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DSSo4pDj5Yf/) in protest of Starbucks' unfair labor practices. The [largest nurses strike in NYC history](https://www.instagram.com/p/DTgS_HDkq1v/) is underway, and Mayor Zohran was [right there on the picket line](https://youtu.be/l01iV3iei7w?si=0GqpHZpt4UTEmuXp) in solidarity with 15,000 nurses and many NYC DSA members.

Socialist elected officials are leading and [winning on an affordability agenda for all,](https://jacobin.com/2025/12/democratic-socialism-municipal-state-strategy) and we're continuing to expand toward our ultimate horizon as socialists: [everything for everyone.](https://jacobin.com/2025/11/socialism-mamdani-dsa-organizing-leadership) NYC DSA members have [stacked mayor-elect Zohran Mamdani's transition team](https://www.instagram.com/p/DRxLploEVAw/?img_index=2&igsh=Nm9ta3RsZHA3anpn) — all the better to [deal with the challenges](https://youtu.be/IsUGgUXMvxw?si=CVDCPGf6ciikJw_n) of [building municipal socialism](https://jacobin.com/2025/12/nyc-dsa-mamdani-organizing-strategy) in [America's largest city.](https://www.thenation.com/article/politics/zohran-mamdani-interview-donald-trump-laguardia/) The right wing is [freaking out](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/01/06/nyregion/cea-weaver-mamdani-tweets.html) about DSA member appointments like [tenant organizer Cea Weaver,](https://dissentmagazine.org/article/tenants-on-the-march-an-interview-with-cea-weaver/) but our movement is strong enough to stand strong against [bad faith attacks from the landlord lobby.](https://jacobin.com/2026/01/mamdani-weaver-housing-landlords-race/) Within two weeks of taking office, mayor Mamdani began to [deliver on a core campaign promise for free childcare for all.](https://www.nyc.gov/mayors-office/news/2026/01/mayor-mamdani---governor-hochul-to-launch-free-child-care-for-tw) We're not resting on our laurels — DSA chapters are looking ahead to [build on our historic wins from the past year](https://2025.electoral.dsausa.org/) and [keep contesting exciting races for elected office across the country in 2026.](https://www.nytimes.com/2025/12/21/opinion/dsa-zohran-mamdani-new-orleans.html)

All this is why more people are part of DSA now than ever. This week we [just passed 95,000 members nationwide,](https://www.instagram.com/p/DTf-k9EjkRN/) our biggest membership milestone yet, with hundreds more plugging in week by week. [Our 2025 recruitment drive](https://falldrive.dsausa.org/dashboard) wrapped right after New Year's Day, and the results show we're organizing everywhere. Some of our fastest growing DSA chapters are in places you might not expect — [Corpus Christi DSA](https://www.instagram.com/p/DSYISw6j9QS/) in Texas; [Sonoma County DSA](https://www.instagram.com/p/DSLqpELkgOy/) in California; [Middle Georgia DSA,](https://www.instagram.com/p/DSXs02qABwk/) and [Bluegrass DSA](https://www.instagram.com/p/DSWXuvXDuec/) in Kentucky. [If you are not yet a DSA member, join us now!](https://act.dsausa.org/donate/membership/)

2026 will demand even more energy and courage from all of us, and we know DSA members everywhere will do the most to support each other in the struggles ahead. With our massive increase in membership comes a lot more in collective resources we can use to level up our people power. At our National Political Committee's first monthly meeting of 2026 last weekend, we committed to major increases in support to the amazing work happening across DSA, with $1 million in direct grant funding we will disburse to chapters throughout the year to sustain and promote our ongoing growth, hiring more full-time staff to support our member-led organizing work, stipends to help support working class leadership of our elected body, and organizing tools to help reach masses of people in our organizing campaigns.

DSA is more powerful today than most of us could have imagined a decade ago, and we will keep organizing everywhere for the world we know we all deserve to win.

In Solidarity,

Ashik and Megan
DSA National Political Committee Co-Chairs

## Apply for the National Arms Embargo Committee and Abolish ICE Committee! Deadline Tuesday 1/27

As part of the implementation of convention resolutions, last December the NPC chartered two new national appointed committees, the [Arms Embargo Committee](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Xmc-pme5__ZdipIk7TfOi7WloV9J1eK6n39Fte-AEGU/edit?tab=t.0) and the [Abolish ICE Committee.](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1aKuiKSnJra_r6x8U4OkJCaQBleLIEO39Ri5gPSPpMvI/edit?usp=sharing)

The Arms Embargo Committee will include four liaisons from other national bodies and At five Large seats. It will provide coordination and develop joint strategies across different bodies carrying out arms embargo-related work.

The Abolish ICE Committee will include five liaisons from other national bodies and six At Large seats, and will develop a robust national priority Abolish ICE campaign.

The NPC is soliciting applications for the At Large seats of both committees! Apply by Tuesday 1/27 midnight PT through the [Arms Embargo Committee Application Form](https://forms.gle/kvmX8J4kUEfmpW1B9) or the [Abolish ICE Committee Application Form](https://forms.gle/C2DgdWBQPQH8DWJu7) if you have relevant experience and are excited to develop DSA's anti-war or migrant defense national strategies.

## Be Part of the DSA National Editorial Board! Applications due TONIGHT, Thursday 1/15

[Applications are DUE TONIGHT to the 2025-2027 DSA National Editorial Board.](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScb5fX9nXqOvr7EkIeYgqi6gwF1haHEuavBQ8eWkllRddmpzQ/viewform) The Editorial Board is a 9-member body appointed by the NPC that oversees the organization's two national publications, Democratic Left and Socialist Forum. The Editorial Board is composed of members with various points of view on important political questions. It does not exist to develop a single theoretical or strategic perspective. As a result, the publications reflect the wide range of views within the organization. The goal of the Editorial Board is not to espouse a particular "party line," but to maintain strong editorial standards for our publications. As such, the process prioritizes familiarity with DSA and editorial experience in appointment to positions on the board.

[If you are interested in being appointed to the Editorial Board, please fill out this application by tonight, Thursday, 1/15 at 11:59PM Hawaii Standard Time.](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScb5fX9nXqOvr7EkIeYgqi6gwF1haHEuavBQ8eWkllRddmpzQ/viewform)
